Sage Waters / Info

So, you're looking for more information about Sage Waters.

First and foremost, you can find his work at Sagewaters.com:

  • For his writings and drawings, head to Words & Pictures.
  • For sculptures and reliefs, look at Shapes & Figures.
  • For film and sound work, there's Sound & Moving.
  • Finally, for his miscellaneous musings, it's Ways & Means.

If you want to purchase some of his work, you can go to directly to his Workshop, online or in person, and see what's for sale.

If you want to buy him a cup of coffee, glass of wine, or a used book, or to become a monthly patron, you can go to the Patrons area.

There's also his obligatory space on the Saatchi platform.

Not one for talking small, he can nevertheless can also be found on Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, and a handful of other hateful platforms where folks quietly build, brand, and market their fragile identities. But don't expect much.

(Sage also manages a Polymer Clay Sculpting group on Facebook, but only as a steward of sorts. He doesn't participate, but don't let that stop you from doing so.)

And that's about it for Sage's presence on the interwebs. If you would like to get in touch with him for some reason, you can do so here.

Get along now, you have appointments to keep.
